--- id: flameless-burning-of-hexamine-fuel-tablet-with-cr2o3-as-a-catalyst mel_id: MEL000006 type: experiment experiment_name: Flameless burning of hexamine fuel tablet with Cr2O3 as a catalyst category: burning reaction: (CH2)6N4 + 9O2 =[Cr2O3]= 6CO2↑ + 2N2↑ + 6H2O↑ description: | Take a hexamine fuel tablet and put some chromium(III) oxide powder on top of that. Set the fuel tablet on fire to heat up the Cr2O3 powder. Then cover the fuel tablet with a glass to stop the fire but not long enough to allow the Cr2O3 poweder to cool down. Now remove the glass. Now the Cr2O3 poweder will heat even stronger. It will stay red-hot until the hexamine fuel tablet is spent.
